Types of Mutual Aid

Food & Nutrition: Primary focus is on nutrition and health. Generally have food pantries and ways to request food.

Rental Assistance: Primary focus is on rental payments for those unable to make rent by their set deadlines.

Benefits Assistance: Primary focus is on assisting veterans with federal and state benefits while also providing multiple resources to assist them.

Utilities Assistance: Primary focus is on assisting on payments towards utilities such as water, gas and electricity.

Emotional Support: Primary focus is on providing emotional support and a safe space for those seeking a group tot alk and interact to about personal issues.
